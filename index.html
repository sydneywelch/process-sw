Design Challenge

The desgin challenge presented for this project was to research an non-profit or charity organization and redesign their website! The non-profit I chose is called You Can Discover Change. They are located in Fort Mill, South Carolina and work with women who are both spiritually and physically incarcerated, as well as women who are dealing with abuse and addiction, through mentorship and teaching programs.

Research

Both Primary and Secondary research were needed for this project in order to know more about the organization, as well as the people who run and support it. By doing this research I was better able to understand the main goals that the organization's website were trying to communicate to the viewer/user.
