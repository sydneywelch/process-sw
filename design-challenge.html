Design Challenge

Below are questions that helped me define this design challenge.

What is the Problem you are trying to solve?

Helping woman who have dealt with incarceration, abuse, and addiction.

1. Frame it as a design question.

How does the YCDC website contribute to helping woman with incarceration, abuse, and addiction?

2. Now, state the ultimate impact you're trying to have.

Increased support, donations, and awareness of woman who have been incarcerated, abused, or addicted to a substance.

3. What are some possible solutions to your problem?

A more consistent design through heirarchy, type choices, and color, organization of information avalible, a clear mission statement and theme throuhgout the website.

4. Finally, write down some of the context and constraints you're facing.

Located in Fort Mill, South Carolina. This is where I am from but I do not live here when in school so communication may be difficult. There is not much to go off of other than the website itself so outside sources may limit me in some capacities.

5. Does your original question need a tweek? Try it again.

How can the YCDC aid in assisting woman in Fort Mill, South Carolina who have a relation to abuse, incarceration, and/or addiction.
